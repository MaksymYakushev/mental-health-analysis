# Mental Health Dashboard

## **Project overview**

This Tableau dashboard provides an interactive analysis of mental health patterns in a remote work environment, offering insights into stress levels, burnout, productivity, and work-life balance across different demographics and regions.

<img src="https://github.com/MaksymYakushev/mental-health-analysis/blob/main/images/project-preview/dashboard.png" width="900" height="650">  

## **Business Context**

Companies and organizations increasingly monitor employee wellbeing to understand the impact of remote work on mental health and productivity. This project visualizes these patterns, allowing stakeholders to explore:

- How stress, burnout, and work-life balance vary across employees
- Whether remote, hybrid, or onsite work affects mental wellbeing
- How factors like workload, sleep, and physical activity relate to mental health
- Differences across ages and demographic groups

## **Dashboard Features**
✅ **Interactive Filters** – Users can filter all dashboard data by age group, work format, department, and region for more detailed analysis.  
✅ **Summary KPIs** – Displays key metrics such as total number of employees, average weekly working hours, sleep quality, and the share of employees without access to mental health resources.  
✅ **Demographics** – A set of visualizations (line, donut, and bar charts) showing the distribution of employees by age, gender, and geographic region.  
✅ **Years of Work** – A heatmap matrix that tracks employee tenure across different groups, with a focus on those with 10+ years of experience.  
✅ **Working Conditions & Productivity** – Bar charts illustrating overtime levels and percentage changes in employee productivity.  
✅ **Mental Health Conditions** – Horizontal charts displaying the prevalence of key conditions such as burnout, depression, and anxiety.  
✅ **Stress Level Analysis** – Detailed tables and pie charts analyzing stress levels based on employment type (part-time/full-time) and access to mental health support.  
✅ **Wellbeing Metrics** – Visualizations of physical activity frequency, as well as ratings of social isolation and work-life balance.  

## **Tools & Techniques**

- **Data Source** – mental health dataset (Kaggle).   
- **SQL** – data cleaning, validation, and preprocessing.
- **Tableau** – interactive data visualization and dashboard development.  
- **Techniques** – KPI metrics, interactive filters, comparative analysis, and dashboard-based visual exploration.
